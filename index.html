Gym.Bot

A Smart Bot which monitors your diet and gymming

  • Get Started

Calorie Counter

Helps you monitor your diet.

Using Nutrionix's API and vast database of food choices, the bot will ask you what you ate for breakfast, lunch, dinner and maintain a count of calories you have intaked and how close you are to your calorie goal of the day.

Gymming

Creates a Gym Plan tailored to your body.

Using your body details and depending on your goals the bot creates a weekly gym plan for you by using data from BodyBuilding.com.

Whether you want to Lose weight, become Lean, or Bulk up we got you covered with our programs.

Accessiblity

Making our app accessible to everyone with a smartphone was our goal.

Gym.Bot takes into account the gymming equipments you have access to and builds your gym program according to that.

It also works as a trainer; showing videos of exercise you are trying to do with correct techniques.

The bot can be used with many popular services such as Facebook Messenger, Skype, Slack, Contana, amomng others.

Visuals

Gym.Bot uses beautiful visual represntaions to make it easy for the user to graph their calorie intake and see how active they are everyday of the week.
